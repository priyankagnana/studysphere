Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right of the homepage, fill in your details, and click "Submit".

How can I reset my password?

If you’ve forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions.
